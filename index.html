About

I provide the public with the highest quality tattooing with reasonable price in Baguio City and sourrounding areas. I pride myself on hard work, determination and helping my clients express their personality and individuality to make their outsides match their insides. My best days are the days I fix a tattoo someone was unhappy with. Or cover scars that made them embarrassed. Or do a memorial tattoo that brings them to tears and helps begin their healing process. My favorite tattoos to do are koi and polynesian.

I love caps and shoes and I own a collection of various vintage and new ones. My friends say I'm hilarious but I'm not so sure. My family has been very supportive, whether or not they agree with tattoos. My closest friends let me learn on them, let me try out new techniques, helped me see things with different sets of eyes. My siblings and my father lent me the bodies to be my canvass even though my mother thinks tattoos are the worst thing you can do to your body. They supported me and gave me constructive criticism when I needed a reality check.

I’ve always been interested in tattooing and having my art as the main source of my income. I had just never thought tattooing would be something I would be able to do. I’ve been doubtful on myself and in this business, you need to have a tough skin. But in the end, the industry chose me, and it seems that as long as I keep trying to be better, it will continue to let me be a part of it.
