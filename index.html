ABOUT

I provide the public with the highest quality tattooing with reasonable price in Baguio City and sourrounding areas. I pride myself on hard work, determination and helping my clients express their personality and individuality to make their outsides match their insides. My favorite tattoos to do are oriental and polynesian designs. The best days for me are the days I fix a tattoo someone was unhappy with or cover scars that made them embarrassed or do a memorial tattoo that brings them to tears and helps begin their healing process.

I love hats and shoes and I own a collection of various flat brim hats- vintage and new ones. My friends say I'm hilarious but I'm not so sure. When I'm not getting somebody inked, I help my older brother with creating beautiful furnitures and home fixtures for his clients. During my downtime, you can almost always find me with my bros playing pool or biking accross benguet mountains or gathered around a bonfire drinking booze under the stars and sometimes, amusing myself with our pet monkey named Vanessa. Yes, I am single (and happy) BUT ready to mingle- (disclaimer: this is only applicable once the pandemic is over).


My family has been very supportive, whether or not they agree with tattoos. My closest friends let me learn on them, let me try out new techniques, helped me see things with different sets of eyes. My siblings and my father lent me their skin to be my canvass even though my mother thinks tattoos are the worst thing you can do to your body. They supported me and gave me constructive criticism when I needed a reality check.


Tattooing has always been my interest and having my love of art as another source of income is such a great privilege. I had just never thought it would be something I would be able to do.
