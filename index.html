ABOUT

I provide the public with quality tattooing at a reasonable price in Baguio City and sorrounding areas. I pride myself on hard work, determination - helping my clients express their personality and individuality to make their outsides match their insides. My favorite projects are oriental and Polynesian designs. The best days for me are the days I fix a tattoo someone was unhappy with or cover scars that made them embarrassed or do a memorial tattoo that brings them to tears and helps begin their healing process.


On 2013, I created my very first homemade tattoo machine. Everyday, over the years, I practiced on my self and later on, my closest friends let me learn on them, try out new techniques and helped me see things with different sets of eyes. My family has been very supportive, whether or not they agree with tattoos. My siblings and my father lent me their skin to be my canvas even though my mother thinks tattoos are the worst thing you can do to your body. They supported me and gave me constructive criticism when I needed a reality check. For the length of my career, I am fortunate enough to have clients locally and clients coming from different continents who believed in my talent as an artist.


Tattooing has always been my interest and having my love of art as another source of income is such a great privilege. I had just never thought it would be something I would be able to do. I had doubts about myself and in this business, you need to have a tough skin. But in the end, the industry chose me, and it seems that as long as I keep trying to be better, it will continue to let me be a part of it.

I love hats & shoes and I own a collection of various flat brim hats- vintage and new ones. My friends say I'm hilarious but I'm not so sure. When I'm not getting somebody inked, I help my older brother create beautiful furniture and home fixtures for his clients. During my downtime, you can almost always find me with my bros playing pool or biking across Benguet mountains or gathered around a bonfire drinking booze under the stars and sometimes, amusing myself with our pet monkey named Vanessa. Yes, I am single (and happy) BUT ready to mingle- (DISCLAIMER: this is only applicable once the pandemic is over).
